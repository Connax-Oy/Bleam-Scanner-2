Detailed Description

Connection to BLEAM devices.

BLEAM Scanner connects to BLEAM device in order to share the scanned data. 

     For details, please refer to [BLEAM Scanner 2 wiki: Connect](https://github.com/Connax-Oy/Bleam-Scanner-2/wiki/Connect).
